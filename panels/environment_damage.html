Falling

A creature takes 1d6 for every 10 feet fallen, up to a maximum of 20d6.

Suffocating

A creature can hold its breath for a number of minutes equal to its 1 + CON modifier (minimum 30 seconds.)

If a creature runs out of breath, it can survive for a number of rounds equal to its CON modifier (minimum 1 round.) After these rounds, the creature drops to 0 HP and is dying.

A creature dying this way cannot regain HP or be stabilized until it can breathe again.
