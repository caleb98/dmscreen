Attack

Swing with a weapon, shoot a bow, throw a punch, etc.

Cast a Spell

Dash

Double your speed for this turn, including any modifiers.

Disengage

Your movement doesn't provoke attacks of opportunity for this turn.

Dodge

Until the start of your next turn, attacks against you have disadvantage if you can see the attacker. Dexterity saves are made at advantage.

Help

Aid a creature in a task, granting them advantage on the next ability check to complete that task.

Hide

Make a Stealth check to attempt to hide.

Ready

Prepare an action to do upon a decided trigger (ie. activating a trap when an enemy walks through it.) The prepared action takes the place of the creature's reaction for this turn.

Use an Object
